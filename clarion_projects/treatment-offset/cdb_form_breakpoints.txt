* CDB breakpoint script for OffsetForm.dll — live GUI tracing
* Traces button clicks (OFDoCalc, OFDoClear) and variable queries (OFGetVar)
* during interactive form use.
*
* Usage: cdb -G -o -cf cdb_form_breakpoints.txt python run_form_dll.py
sxe ld:OffsetForm
g
.echo *** CDB: OffsetForm.dll loaded, setting breakpoints ***
bu OffsetForm!OFRunForm ".echo TRACE_ENTER OFRunForm; gc"
bu OffsetForm!OFDoCalc ".echo TRACE_ENTER OFDoCalc; gu; .echo TRACE_EXIT OFDoCalc eax=; r eax; gc"
bu OffsetForm!OFDoClear ".echo TRACE_ENTER OFDoClear; gu; .echo TRACE_EXIT OFDoClear eax=; r eax; gc"
bu OffsetForm!OFGetVar ".echo TRACE_ENTER OFGetVar; .echo   arg1(id)=; dd esp+4 L1; gu; .echo TRACE_EXIT OFGetVar eax=; r eax; gc"
.echo *** CDB: Breakpoints set — interact with the form ***
g
